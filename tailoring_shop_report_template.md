## 3.5 LIMITATIONS OF THE STUDY

### Technical Limitations
1. **Free-Tier Service Constraints**: Dialogflow Essentials limited to 1,000 monthly chatbot interactions, restricting extensive user testing and production scaling
2. **Development Tool Restrictions**: Figma Community Plan limitations on team collaboration and advanced prototyping features
3. **Platform Compatibility**: Android and iOS deployment required separate APK/IPA generation outside the eight-week timeline

### Sample Size Limitations
1. **Geographic Scope**: Study limited to Bengaluru metropolitan area, may not represent all Indian regional tailoring practices and preferences
2. **Time Constraints**: Eight-week duration restricted comprehensive longitudinal user adoption studies and long-term performance monitoring
3. **Sample Representation**: Purposive sampling focused on technology-accessible respondents, potentially excluding most traditional shop owners

### Methodology Limitations
1. **Self-Reported Data**: Survey responses subject to social desirability bias and subjective interpretation
2. **Technology Novelty**: Initial user feedback potentially influenced by novelty effect rather than long-term utility assessment
3. **Performance Benchmarking**: Free-tier service limitations prevented comprehensive load testing and extreme usage scenario validation

### Business Context Limitations
1. **Industry Specificity**: Solution designed specifically for tailoring industry, requiring adaptation for other service sectors
2. **Cultural Context**: Study conducted in urban Bengaluru context, may not fully apply to rural or different cultural settings
3. **Economic Validation**: Cost-benefit analysis validated for small shops only, scalability to larger operations untested
